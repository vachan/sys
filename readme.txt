Show your Support helps you to show your Support for an Event/WordCamp by adding a overlay image to your profile picture on Facebook and Twitter.

== Description ==

Show your Support Plugin is to show your Support for an Event/WordCamp by updating your profile picture on Facebook and Twitter adding a overlay image.

Major features in Show your Support include:

* Change your profile picture on Facebook and Twitter
* Plugin uploads profile picture to your Facebook/Twitter timeline with a support message.
* Facebook doesn't allow plugin to directly change profile picture, You will have to manually set your updated profile picture on your timeline
* Twitter allows to change your profile picture from remove server, So the plugin tweets and changes profile picture accordingly. 
* Use shortcode [ft_sys] on post/page/custom post type

== Installation ==

Upload the Show your Support plugin to your blog, Activate it, then Use shortcode [ft_sys] on post/page/custom post type
